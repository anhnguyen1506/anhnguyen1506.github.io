Traditional

Classic, comfortable and calm - Traditional decorating is calm, orderly, and predictable. There is nothing wild or chaotic: everything has the look that it goes together. It is a style that feels comfortable for any age group, and is a familiar look that you'd see in magazines or furniture showrooms.

English Country

English Country style emphasizes coziness and our relationship with nature. The decor can be casual or formal, with furniture traditionally styled and the rooms full of color. Whether the home is a small cottage or sprawling estate, English County decorating exudes warmth, comfort and a love for family.

Modern French

French interiors provide a stunning blend of sophistication that borrows from a variety of different styles, incorporating bold new styles that blend seamlessly with more traditional designs to provide a chic and stylish palette. The style requires some focus but at the heart of this aesthetic is creating spaces that incorporate an original flair with small splashes of flamboyance.
