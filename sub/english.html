Furniture Pieces


____________________________



In this style, pieces from different time periods and different styles are to be combined: Art Deco, tufted furniture, a chesterfield sofa, wingback chairs - no sets of any kind

Materials


____________________________



A variety of wood is used in this style, including mahogany and walnut. Regarding fabric, velvet, toile, leather, damask, linen and brocade are common. Fabrics usually go with tassels and fringe.

Rugs


____________________________



Common types of rugs used are Oriental, Persian, or Kilim rugs, layered over sisal or jute rugs.

Lighting


____________________________



French empire crystal chandeliers are great for this style. Traditional floor lamps, sconces and table lamps also fit in very well.


Window Treatments


____________________________



The most authentic choice would be either heavy velvet curtains or curtains with rich floral patterns.

Floorings


____________________________



Wood floor is the most suitable choice for this style, giving the place an antique look.

Artworks


____________________________



Common types of artworks used are large portraits, watercolor, landscapes, canine and equine art.

Accessories


____________________________



Besides a fireplace, books, needlepoint pillows, foot stools, stained glass, crystal candleholders, or a beautiful tea set will fit in.
